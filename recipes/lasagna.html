Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish loved by many. With our simple recipe, you'll get the whole family full and happy

Recipe is sourced from: allrecipes' World's Best Lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
